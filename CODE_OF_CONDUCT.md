# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or level of familiarity with agent tooling.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and code rather than people.
- Assume good intent while still asking for evidence.
- Respect privacy and do not publish another person's local paths, Skill contents, logs, or security reports.
- Make space for people who communicate in different languages or styles.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, sustained disruption, and retaliation are not tolerated in project spaces.

## Scope and enforcement

This policy applies in issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, community channels, and public events where someone represents the project. Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through a repository-owned private contact or moderation channel. Maintainers may remove content, issue a warning, temporarily restrict participation, or permanently ban participation when necessary. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.
