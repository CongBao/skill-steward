# Alpha Testing Protocol

1. Ask the participant to run `skill-steward scan` without explaining individual findings.
2. Record time to first finding they consider useful.
3. Ask them to label every warning or higher finding as useful, incorrect, unclear, or already-known.
4. Ask which one action they would take if safe undo were available.
5. Record objections to local scanning, stored reports, hooks, and optional outcome telemetry separately.
6. Do not upload the report. The participant may share a redacted copy voluntarily.

Phase 1 advances only after at least 100 findings from at least 20 portfolios are labeled and actionable precision is at least 60%.
